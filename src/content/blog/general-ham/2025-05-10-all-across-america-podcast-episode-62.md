I’ll admit it—I was a bit apprehensive about stepping behind the mic for a podcast. I consider myself a pretty humble person, and talking about _myself_ isn’t exactly my usual mode. But I’m glad I did.

I recently had the pleasure of joining Marc Vig on the **[All Across America](<https://allacrossamerica.com/>)** podcast. What started as a bit outside my comfort zone turned into a genuinely enjoyable conversation. We talked about my time in the **Air Force** , the unique **[history of Wake Island](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wake_Island>)** , what it’s like to **live and work here** , and of course, a little **ham radio** action too.

Marc made it easy—his curiosity and thoughtful questions really brought the story to life. If you've ever wondered what day-to-day life is like on a remote island in the Pacific, or how ham radio fits into the picture, I think you’ll enjoy it.
